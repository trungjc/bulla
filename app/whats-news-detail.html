our new NUT FREE* ice cream

2nd August 2017 1:41 pm

Available from 14th August 2017 at Woolworths & Independent Supermarkets (including Tasmania)

Bulla is the first manufacturer in Australia to produce nut free ice cream made with fresh Australian milk and cream, to meet the needs of families affected by nut and gluten allergies.
Bulla works with an Independent lab that tests incoming ingredients and finished products to ensure everything is nut free.
