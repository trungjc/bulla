serves: 8
share this recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pkt orange sanding sugar
  • 1 tube green icing
  • 1 tube black icing
  • 250 g vanilla icing coloured orange
  • orange food colouring
  • 1 pkt black sanding sugar
  • 1 tube black icing
  • orange smarties, for eyes
  • rope liquorice, for legs

Spooky Sangas

method

  1. For the pumpkins, Spread top of sandwich with the orange icing, then dip sandwich in the orange sugar to coat icing. Roll sides of sandwich in sugar.
  2. Pipe on black stripes and then a stalk in green icing. Freeze until ready to serve.
  3. For the Spiders, Spread top of sandwich with the black icing. Dip into black sugar to coat icing and coat sides of sandwich in sugar.
  4. Using the same icing, attach 2 orange candies and dot with black to form eyes. Cut strings of liquorice and attach 6 for the spiders legs
