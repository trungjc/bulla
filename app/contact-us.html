  • Are your products Australian made?

    Bulla Family Dairy remains one of Australia’s largest family owned dairy companies and our products are made from 100% Australian milk and cream.



  • Are your products Gluten Free?

    Our cottage cheese, cream, sour cream, crème fraiche and yogurts are all free of gluten.

    We also have a wide range of frozen products that are free of gluten. Please contact our customer service team on Free Call 1800 001 332 or by emailing service@bulla.com.au for a complete list.

  • Are your products Nut Free?

    Bulla is the first manufacturer in Australia to produce nut free*/gluten free ice cream made with fresh Australian milk and cream, to meet the needs of families affected by nut and gluten allergies. Bulla employs the use of a dedicated facility to manufacture the nut free product, this respective facility has dedicated and educated staff that are made aware of Bulla’s nut free environment to ensure that it remains safe.
     Bulla has developed an intense testing program to ensure the nut free product is never compromised. Bulla works with an Independent NATA accredited laboratory that tests incoming ingredients and finished products to ensure everything is nut free.
     We have a group of carefully picked suppliers that are as committed as us to making this product available.
     Bulla is supported by Allergy & Anaphylaxis Australia in its commitment to allergen safety.
     For more information regarding allergies visit www.allergyfacts.org.au

    *made in a factory that does not use nuts and tests for the presence of peanuts, walnut, almond, cashew, hazelnut, macadamia & pistachio but cannot test for the presence of other types of nuts.

  • Are your products Pasteurised?

    The short answer is “Yes”! In Australia all dairy products are required by law to undergo a heat treatment known as pasteurisation.

  • Are your products Vegetarian friendly?

    Some of our Bulla products are not suitable for vegetarians as they contain gelatine which is derived from Bovine and/or colour 120 which is derived from beetle - Cochineal, Carmines.

  • Are your cows 100 % grass or grain fed?

    Bulla utilises milk from various parts of South West Victoria. A combination of both grass feed and grain feed methods are used which is largely dependent on the time of year. We practice ethical sourcing as a part of our procurement policy and we ensure animal welfare is a part of this practice.

  • Are your cows treated with antibiotics or hormones?

    It is standard practise to treat cows that have infections with antibiotics. With this in mind, our suppliers have a number of protocols and procedures to ensure that cows treated are not being milked. In addition to this Bulla tests and checks for the presence of antibiotics in all incoming milk and cream supplies and as a precautionary measure to ensure no antibiotic milk is utilised within our manufacturing process.

  • Can I buy products directly from you?

    Unfortunately, we do not sell direct to the public.

  • Can I freeze your Creams?

    It is recommended that our Bulla Creams are not frozen as it may affect the taste, texture and quality of the cream.

  • Do your products contain Alcohol?

    Our retail frozen products contain extremely small traces of alcohol in the flavouring used, which is derived from Ethanol.

  • Do your products contain Genetically Modified Material?

    The Food Standards Code requires that the label on all packaging of genetically modified food must include a statement ‘Genetically Modified’ in conjunction with the name of that food or ingredient or processing aid. Bulla products are considered to not contain Genetically Modified Food and you can be assured of this by simply checking the label on our products.

  • Do your products contain Eggs?

    Due to the introduction of egg used in our Frozen Custard range, we have included the statement “May Contain Egg” on our packaging for those products that may be affected by this allergen.

  • Do your products contain Palm Oil?

    Bulla shares the concerns of all Australians about the impacts of deforestation and we are continually exploring ways to reduce and eliminate the sourcing of palm oil derived ingredients from our supply chain.

    To that end Bulla supports the production of sustainable palm oil and is a member of the Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO). The RSPO is a non-profit group that promotes palm oil production practices that help to reduce deforestation.

    Bulla has RSPO mass balance certification which means that a guaranteed proportion of any palm oil we use is from RSPO certified plantations. Bulla is currently investigating non palm oil options and certified sustainable segregated palm oil options for our products.

  • Do your products contain Soy?

    Soy is used in some of our products and we have included the statement “May Contain Soy” on our packaging for those products that may be affected by this allergen.

  • How do I use your Creams?

    Bulla Cooking Cream:
    Bulla Cooking Cream has been specifically developed for cooking, it heats without curdling, resists splitting and rapidly thickens, making it a perfect addition to curries, casseroles and pasta sauces.

    Bulla Crème Fraiche:
    Considered to be the most flexible and multipurpose cream available, it is perfect for sweet and savoury dishes and does not break or curdle when cooked. Crème Fraiche is a perfect addition to soups, casseroles or sauces.

    Bulla Dollop Cream:
    Dollop cream is perfect for serving with desserts such as scones and can be used straight from the tub without whipping. It is also ideal for filling cakes and pastries and serving with desserts.

    Bulla Pure Cream:
    Pure cream is a pouring style cream that has 45% fat content and made with nothing but Pure Cream. It is a rich, creamy & decedent cream, perfect for whipping, cooking or simply pouring over your favourite dessert.

    Bulla Thickened Cream:
    A great all-purpose cream that is easy to whip with a stable hold and is also suitable for cooking. Thickened cream is ideal for desserts, cake filling or serving with scones.

    Bulla Whipping Cream:
    Bulla Whipping Cream is created specifically for whipping. This cream whips in just 90 seconds and holds its shape longer once whipped. Ideal for decorating cakes, Pavlovas and other delicious desserts.

  • How long will the products last once opened?

    Providing the product is stored at the correct temperature indicated on the packaging and has not been contaminated or compromised by any other food, it should last until the use by date.

  • What are your Emulsifiers derived from?

    Emulsifiers 471, 476 and 477 are derived from vegetable.

  • What is your Gelatine derived from?

    The Gelatine in our products is sourced from Australia and is derived from Bovine (cattle).

  • What are your Thickeners derived from?

    Thickeners 407A and 412 are derived from vegetable.

  • What Cultures are used in your Sour Cream?

    Contains cultures Lactococcus spp. and Streptococcus thermophiles.

  • What Cultures are used in your Yogurt?

    Contains cultures Lactobacillus, Streptococcus and Bifidobacterium.
